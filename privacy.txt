We do not collect, store, or process any personal data from users of our Services.

Name

Phone number

not to have them stored on your device.

We do not store any personal data, but we still take reasonable 
steps to ensure that our Services are secure and free from unauthorized access, 
use, or disclosure. While we cannot guarantee complete security, we strive to 
protect the information you share with us.

6. Children's Privacy
Our Services are not intended for use by children under the age of 13. 
We do not knowingly collect any personal data from children. If we discover 
that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 13,
